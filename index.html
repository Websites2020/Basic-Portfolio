About Me

I'm from Cleveland, Ohio, but grew up in the Washington, DC area. I majored in urban studies/regional planning at Cleveland State University. I'm new to programming, but have always wanted to learn how it's done. My interest in web development started a long time ago, but I never really got around to it. I'm starting now because I just graduated from college and have some free time that I am deciding to spend on improving my design and problem solving skills. I currently work at a consulting firm focused on managing the tarrif rate quotas of rice being imported from the United States into the European Union and Colombia.


My current skills are using ArcGIS mapping software and Microsoft Office.


What I hope to get out of this class is to take what I learn and build my own web applications. I think that would be really cool and I admire people who can do this.
